Final Recommendation

If I were leading this project as a product designer, I would not aim for "another Laravel project." I would aim for a marketplace that feels production-ready. The benchmark would be the polish of Airbnb's search flow, Stripe's dashboards, Apple's visual restraint, and Google's Material Design interaction patterns—adapted into a cohesive experience tailored specifically for bike rentals. Every screen should prioritize clarity, trust, speed, and ease of use, so the application looks and behaves like a real commercial product rather than a student assignment.
